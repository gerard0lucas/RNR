Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2025

At RNR Estates, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our real estate services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide when inquiring about properties or services.
  • Property Preferences: Information about the type of property you are interested in, budget range, and location preferences.
  • Website Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers when you access our website.
  • Cookies: Data stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide real estate services and property information
  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Send property recommendations based on your preferences
  • Process property transactions and documentation
  • Improve our website and services based on usage data
  • Comply with legal obligations related to real estate transactions

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Property owners and developers when necessary to facilitate property transactions
  • Service providers who assist us in our business operations (such as document processing)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Financial institutions involved in property transactions with your consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

As our valued client, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information we have collected
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information where applicable
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Retention Period

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or for legitimate business purposes related to real estate transactions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for any updates.
